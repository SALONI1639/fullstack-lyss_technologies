"Why is a Resume Important for student's?"

A student resume is a summary of a student's academic and personal experiences, skills, and achievements.
It can be used to apply for college, jobs, internships, scholarships, or recommendations. What to include Contact information: Include your name and professional contact details Objective: State your career goals and how the position you're applying for will support them Education: List your education, including any relevant coursework or grades Skills: Highlight skills like teamwork, communication, and problem-solving Experience: Include any relevant work experience, volunteer work, or extracurricular activities Resume tips Keep your resume to one page, Use a reverse-chronological format, Organize your text into bullet points,
Tailor your resume to each position you apply for,
and Emphasize your strengths.

What is the main purpose of your resume?

A resume is to give your potential employer a feel for your past experience and skills.
Remember you are trying to sell yourself, so emphasize your strengths.
Most employers look for a steady job history and positive patterns of progression (i.e. increased responsibility, promotions).

Types of resumes

  1. chronological
  2. functional
  3. combination

Tips for writing an effective resume.

When applying for a job, a resume is a document that you put together to market your talents and expertise to a hiring manager.
Hiring managers go through numerous resumes to identify individuals that closely fit a companies' requirements.
They normally conduct interviews with prospective candidates to select who to recruit.
